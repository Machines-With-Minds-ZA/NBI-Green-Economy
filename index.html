A GREEN ECONOMY TOOLKIT

South Africa and the world are switching to a green economy, and that is creating a lot of opportunities for businesses in the installation, repair and maintenance (RM) sector. We want to share these opportunities with you, so we've put together this comprehensive green economy toolkit that's a digital tool to help you gain full understanding of what a green economy is, and how your business fits into the growing trend towards green business. Here you will find short explanations, support tools and various initiatives that are giving helping hands to green economy, we've also put together a step-by-step guide on how to access the benefits of a green economy, and information where to go for support.

Preparing your business for the green economy

For your business

The world is moving towards a green economy, and South Africa must do so if it is to continue trading internationally. Our existing buildings and infrastructure must be adapted to make the way they are operated more sustainable, and new infrastructure must be built according to green principles, so it does not use more natural resources than absolutely necessary. A lot of work needs to be done.

This is where you come in. But how do you make sure you can get some of this work?

Find out more

For your future

The South African government has chosen nine areas to focus on in the switch to a green economy; agriculture; energy (clean and efficient); natural resource conservation and management (including mining); sustainable transport and infrastructure; environmental sustainability (incl tourism education); green building and the built environment; sustainable waste management and recycling; water management; and sustainable production and consumption.
