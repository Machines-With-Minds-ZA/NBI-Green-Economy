Access to learning opportunities

The National Business Initiative (NBI) has created an online application to improve the learning experience for technical and business-related topics. Key features of the app include offline content, the ability to download management system when connected, self-paced learning, progress tracking, and tracking of learning progress.

This app is part of the Installation, Repair, and Maintenance (IRM) Initiative, which jobs, the initiative is a collaboration between the NBI, government and the private sector, offering demand-led skills training and workplace-based learning and enterprise development with technology integration for a more dynamic learning process.

You can download the app or provided for downloadable content on your mobile device.
